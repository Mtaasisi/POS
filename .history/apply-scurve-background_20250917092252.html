Method 1: Browser Console (Quickest)

Open your browser's developer console (F12) and paste this code:

// Apply S-Curve Red & Blue background document.body.classList.remove('bg-gradient-to-br', 'from-blue-50', 'to-indigo-100'); document.body.classList.add('s-curve-red-blue'); localStorage.setItem('selectedWallpaper', 's-curve-red-blue'); console.log('S-Curve Red & Blue background applied!');
